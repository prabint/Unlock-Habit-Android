# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** July 13, 2026

Unlock Habit ("the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how I
handle your information.

## 1. Information Collection and Use

**No Personal Data Collection:** I do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information, such
as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

**No Account Required:** You can use the App without creating an account or providing any
registration information.

**Local Storage:** The App stores your preferences (such as your selected habit app) locally on your
device using Android's DataStore and Room database. This information never leaves your device and is
used solely for the App's functionality.

## 2. Permissions

The App requires certain permissions to function:

- **Notifications:** Used to maintain a foreground service required by Android to monitor device
  unlock events.
- **Display over other apps:** Used solely to bring the App to the foreground so it can launch your
  selected habit app upon device unlock.

These permissions are used only for the core functionality of the App and do not involve collecting
your data.

## 3. Data Sharing

**No Data Sharing:** I do not share any data with third parties. Since I do not collect any data,
there is no information to share, sell, or trade.

## 4. Advertising

**No Ads:** The App does not contain any advertisements and does not use any advertising identifiers
or tracking.

## 5. Security

I value your trust in using this App. Since all data is stored locally on your device, its security
is protected by your device's built-in security features.

## 6. Open Source

The App is open source, and you can review the source code
on [GitHub](https://github.com/prabint/Unlock-Habit-Android).

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

I may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an
updated effective date.

## 8. Contact

If you have any questions or suggestions about this Privacy Policy, feel free to open an issue on
the [GitHub repository](https://github.com/prabint/Unlock-Habit-Android) or contact me.
